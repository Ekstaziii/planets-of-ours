planets of ours

What are those?

planets of ours is an NFT project inspired by the solid beauties of the universe - the planets and their moons

Who created them?

these planets were drawn by me in an attempt to express my passion for the distant and unknown, the cosmos and its majesty

Who is 'me'?

i am an ambitious individual which you can refer to as 'eccy' / 'eccyboo' - the names comes from 'ecstasy' i.e. extreme delight

Will there be more?

yes, another set of seemingly uninhabitable set of planets is yet to be explored (and drawn)
